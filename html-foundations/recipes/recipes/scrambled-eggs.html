Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled butt-nuggies

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/4c half and half
  • 1/4tsp Kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • black pepper
  • fresh chopped herbs

Instructions

  1. Aggressivly whisk eggs, half and half, and salt until mixture is uniform in texture, and light and foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a small nonstick pan, coating the whole pan
  3. Add eggs to middle of pan and immediately reduce to low heat
  4. wait for edges to start to set, then using a spatula, move all the egg around in the pan, allowing uncooked egg to settle and cook
  5. When eggs are mostly cooked with large pillowy blocks, slow bold eggs into themselves in the middle of the pan
  6. Remove from heat - eggs will shimmer with some moisture
  7. Plate, add herbs, and salt/pepper to taste
  8. Serve!
